  • Known vs. Unknown (from agent point of view - we know position of agent, etc)

  • Dynamic vs. Static (during agent thinking the world is changing/unchanging)

  • Episodic vs. Sequential

  • Stochastic vs. Deterministic

  • Noiseless vs. Noisy

  • Discrete vs. Continuous

I am really happy user of Pocketbook 623. Why?
Because I can connect to Eduroam (tested in Prague domain: cuni.cz) now!

Pocketbook 623 correctly recognized 

Security: WPA/WPA2 enterprise
EAP type: PEAP TLS TTLS
I just filled 

username: my_private_login@cuni.cz
password: my_secure_password

Click connect and you are in!

I ignore the certificates. I would recommend to use them. However, I use the device only at school and I suppose that there are no faked hotspots. 


Software version: W623.4.4.252

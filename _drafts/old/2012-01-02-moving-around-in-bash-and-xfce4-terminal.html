Shortcuts I know (and forget:))



  • Completition 

        Tab

  • Go to beggining of line

        Ctrl+A

  • Clear everything to the end of line

        Ctrl+K

  • Clear line

        Ctrl+U

  • Reverse history

        Ctrl+R

  • Clear last word

        Ctrl+W

  • Freeze shell-disables scrolling. Pretty awful during using vi. It looks like vi is not responding.

        Ctrl+S

  • Resume shell-resume scrolling. The rescue usually when vi is frozen!

        Ctrl+Q


Please let me know more!
